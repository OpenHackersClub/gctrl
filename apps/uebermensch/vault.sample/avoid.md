# Styles I avoid

- Hype language — "historic", "unprecedented", "game-changer", "TAM is infinite".
- Unverified casualty figures from a single social-media post. Wait for AP/Reuters wire.
- Single-analyst-opinion framings presented as fact. Attribute or skip.
- Restated public statements without primary-source links.
- Speculation about internal regime dynamics without named intelligence reporting.
- Partisan framings (left or right) — reach for the dryest factual summary available.
