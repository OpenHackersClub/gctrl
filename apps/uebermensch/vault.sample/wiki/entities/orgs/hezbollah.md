# Hezbollah

Lebanese Shia paramilitary + political party, historically the cornerstone of [[iran]]'s "axis of resistance". Assessed as structurally collapsed by April 2026.

## Degradation timeline (per Belfer Center analysis)

- **Oct 8 2023** — entered Gaza conflict alongside Hamas; began rocket fire against Israel.
- **2023–24** — Israel conducted 9,812 strikes inside Lebanon; Hezbollah fired ~15,400 rockets (roughly 18% at non-evacuated targets; ~90% deep strikes intercepted).
- **Sep 2024** — Hassan Nasrallah assassinated; IRGC forced into greater operational involvement.
- **Jan 2025** — Lebanon elected first president without Iranian/Syrian imposition; constitutional amendments affirmed state monopoly on arms.
- **Mar 2026** — intervention during [[iran]]'s Operation Epic Fury accelerated organizational collapse.
- **Apr 16 2026** — Israel–Lebanon 10-day truce begins ([[iran-israel-escalation]]).

## Residual capability

- Mostly short-range missiles. Medium-range arsenal estimated at ~1/5 pre-war level.
- Reconstruction cost estimated by World Bank at $11B — exceeds combined Iran–Hezbollah capacity ([[2026-04-18--belfer-proxy-degradation]]).
