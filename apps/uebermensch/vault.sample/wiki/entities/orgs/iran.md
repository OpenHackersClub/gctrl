# Islamic Republic of Iran

Target of the Feb 28 2026 combined US–Israeli strike. Supreme Leader [[ali-khamenei]] killed in opening phase; succeeded by Mojtaba Khamenei. Other senior casualties include military commanders Abdolrahim Mousavi and Aziz Nasirzadeh, plus official Ali Larijani.

## Current posture

- Military: drone + ballistic missile capacity significantly degraded. Iran fired hundreds of drones and missiles at Israel + US bases in Bahrain, Jordan, Kuwait, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, UAE in the opening weeks ([[2026-04-18--wikipedia-2026-iran-war]]).
- Political: accepted Apr 8 ceasefire via Pakistan mediation, rejected subsequent US terms on Apr 6.
- Nuclear: terminated [[iaea]] access Feb 28 2026; DNI says enrichment not resumed but unverifiable ([[2026-04-18--armscontrol-iran-nuclear-status]]).
- Proxy network: structurally degraded across [[hezbollah]], [[houthis]], Iraqi militias ([[2026-04-18--belfer-proxy-degradation]]).
