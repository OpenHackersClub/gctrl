# State of Israel

Co-belligerent with United States in Operation Roaring Lion / Operation Epic Fury against [[iran]] from Feb 28 2026. PM [[benjamin-netanyahu]] leads; FM Israel Katz on diplomatic track.

## Current posture

- Military: 9,812 strikes inside Lebanon during 2023–24 per Belfer Center ([[2026-04-18--belfer-proxy-degradation]]); continued operations vs. [[hezbollah]] through early 2026.
- Diplomatic: agreed Apr 16 2026 10-day Lebanon truce — Netanyahu framed it as "advancing peace efforts".
- Strategic: proxy degradation (especially [[hezbollah]]) is a core achievement; nuclear ambiguity in [[iran]] is the unresolved residual risk.
