# Natanz nuclear facility

Primary uranium-enrichment site of [[iran]], operated by the Atomic Energy Organization of Iran (AEOI). Includes the main Fuel Enrichment Plant (FEP) and the Pilot Fuel Enrichment Plant (PFEP). Struck in the opening phase of [[iran-israel-escalation]] (Feb 28 2026).

## Centrifuge inventory (pre-strike)

- 42 cascades of IR-1 centrifuges.
- 37 IR-2 cascades (15 operating).
- 13 operating IR-4 cascades.
- 15 IR-6 cascades (7 functional).

## Post-strike status

Unknown. [[iaea]] has no access since Feb 28 2026 ([[2026-04-18--armscontrol-iran-nuclear-status]]). Enrichment-knowledge component cannot be reversed by kinetic strike — only physical centrifuge + stockpile destruction translates to breakout delay.
