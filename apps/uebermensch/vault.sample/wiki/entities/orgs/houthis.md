# Ansar Allah (Houthi movement)

Yemeni Zaidi Shia armed movement, Iran-aligned "axis of resistance" member. The most resilient Iranian proxy as of April 2026, though strategically retrenching.

## Posture timeline

- **2023–24** — leveraged Gaza conflict to assert regional relevance via Red Sea shipping attacks and threats to Eilat.
- **2025** — US Operation Rough Rider (~1,100 airstrikes); Israel killed Houthi cabinet members Aug 2025. FTO designation re-applied Mar 2025.
- **Dec 2023 → renewed 2025** — US sanctions designations; financial strangulation via strikes on Hodeida Port fuel storage (~$20M direct losses).
- **Late 2025** — Abdul Malik al-Houthi withdrew from public visibility; "axis of resistance" rhetoric abandoned in favor of "secondary supporter" framing.
- **Feb 28 2026** — threatened to escalate following [[iran-israel-escalation]] opening strikes.
- **Mar 28 2026** — resumed attacks on Israel; counter-evidence to the Belfer thesis of terminal degradation.

## Strategic assessment

Per Belfer Center ([[2026-04-18--belfer-proxy-degradation]]): effectively ceased regional power projection; prioritizing domestic survival. The May 2025 deal with [[donald-trump]] to stop attacking US ships is a quiet constraint.
