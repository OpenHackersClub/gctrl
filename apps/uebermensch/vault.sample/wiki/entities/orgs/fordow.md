# Fordow Fuel Enrichment Plant

Hardened, mountain-buried enrichment facility near Qom. Primary near-20%+ enrichment site for [[iran]]. Late-2024 announcements indicated plans to scale 60% UF6 production from 4.7 → 37 kg/month; install 32 additional centrifuge cascades.

## Strategic significance

Fordow's depth (~80m of rock cover) made it the hardest non-weapons-grade conventional target in the Feb 28 2026 strike package. US use of GBU-57 MOPs was widely reported pre-war as the only conventional option. Post-strike damage assessment unpublished.
