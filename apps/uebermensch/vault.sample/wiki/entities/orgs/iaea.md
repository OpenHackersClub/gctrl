# International Atomic Energy Agency

UN nuclear watchdog. Primary verification body for [[iran-nuclear-program]]. Access terminated by Iran on Feb 28 2026, creating the most significant verification blackout in IAEA–Iran history.

## Key documents

- **GOV/2025/24** — verification + monitoring in Iran, mid-2025.
- **GOV/2026/8** — Feb 27 2026 board report covering post-strike verification gaps.

## Current posture

- Director General Rafael Grossi has warned against an "illusion of an agreement" lacking verification.
- IAEA has not been provided access to Iranian facilities since the strikes; cannot assess facility or material status ([[2026-04-18--armscontrol-iran-nuclear-status]]).
- Iran's letter of Nov 20 2025 declared the Cairo agreement "no longer valid and shall henceforth be regarded as terminated".
