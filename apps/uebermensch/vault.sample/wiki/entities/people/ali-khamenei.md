# Ali Khamenei

Second Supreme Leader of [[iran]] (1989–2026). **Assassinated Feb 28 2026** in the opening Israeli airstrike on his compound. Iranian state media confirmed the death on Mar 1 2026 ([[2026-04-18--wikipedia-2026-iran-war]]).

Succeeded by Mojtaba Khamenei (son), announced as the new Supreme Leader.

## Pre-war relevance

- Decades of fatwa framework rejecting weaponization while pursuing latent enrichment capability.
- His death removed the long-standing theological brake on weaponization decisions under the Mojtaba succession.
