# Donald Trump

47th President of the United States. Led the US side of Operation Epic Fury against [[iran]] from Feb 28 2026, supported by Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth.

## Statements + actions during the war

- **Apr 1 2026** — address to the nation: strikes have "dramatically curtailed" Iranian missile/drone systems; threatened to escalate "to bring them back to the Stone Ages, where they belong".
- **Apr 6 2026** — threatened to strike Iranian energy infrastructure + bridges absent an Apr 7 deal.
- **Apr 13 2026** — announced US naval blockade of [[strait-of-hormuz]].
- **Apr 17 2026** — expressed optimism about a Tehran deal; said he "might" go to Pakistan if a peace deal were ready to sign.
