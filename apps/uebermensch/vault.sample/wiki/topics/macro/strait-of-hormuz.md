# Strait of Hormuz

Narrow waterway between [[iran]] and Oman — the chokepoint for ~20% of global seaborne oil. Under US naval blockade since Apr 13 2026 per [[donald-trump]]'s announcement.

## Relevance to the escalation

- Iran closed the strait after the Feb 28 2026 strikes, then refused to reopen on Apr 8 after Israeli operations in Lebanon resumed.
- US blockade enforces entry/exit restrictions on ships to/from Iranian ports; effective asymmetric pressure tool ([[2026-04-18--wikipedia-2026-iran-war]]).
- Reopening is the leverage point in the Apr 22 framework negotiations.

## Investable framings

- **Direct** — BZ crude, shipping rates (BDTI), LNG (QatarGas transit risk).
- **Second-order** — MENA-exposed insurers (war-risk premia), refining margins.
- **Third-order** — regional bank equity if escalation resumes.
